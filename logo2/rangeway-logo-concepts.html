Rangeway Logo Explorations

Rough concepts exploring "premium hospitality destination" over "outdoor adventure brand"

Concept A: Shelter on the Horizon

The idea: A single peak that reads as both mountain AND roofline. The warm glow beneath suggests "there's comfort waiting inside." Journey implied by the gentle path line.

Keeps your warm palette but shifts meaning from "wilderness" to "welcoming destination."

Concept B: The Warm Window

The idea: The glowing center is the "window" you see from the road. That warm light drawing you in. The subtle peak inside connects to travel without dominating.

Works as app icon but the meaning is "warmth inside" not "outdoor app."

Concept C: The Path Mark

The idea: A distinctive R lettermark where the leg suggests a road extending forward. The warm dot marks your destination. Simple, ownable, scales beautifully.

Lettermarks often feel more premium and work better at tiny sizes.

Concept D: The Horizon Rest

The idea: Ultra-minimal. A shelter on the horizon with that unmistakable warm doorway. Golden hour sun. Says "your comfortable stop is just ahead."

More illustrative, could work well for signage and environmental applications.
