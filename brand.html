Brand Essence

Rangeway is America's first premium EV charging network designed for scenic highway corridors and National Parks routes. We treat charging as hospitality, not utility, guaranteeing indoor climate-controlled comfort at every location.

Our Promise

Indoor comfort at every location. Clean restrooms, climate control, Wi-Fi, and a place to rest while you charge on America's most beautiful routes.

Network Model

Hub-satellite operations: Basecamp hubs (6-8 DCFC, café, NOC) support Waystations (4 DCFC, lounge, automated retail) for 99% uptime.

Our Travelers

EV road-trippers exploring scenic corridors, adventure travelers visiting National Parks, and locals who value predictable charging experiences.

Brand Voice

Warm and welcoming, not corporate. Confident but not arrogant. Knowledgeable but not condescending. We celebrate the journey and the places that make it memorable.

Writing Principles

Benefits before features, always. Active voice preferred. Short sentences for clarity. Tell stories, don't just list facts. Include calls-to-action naturally.

Logo Usage Guidelines

Clear Space

Maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the "R" in RANGEWAY. For the icon, maintain clear space equal to 25% of its width on all sides.

✓ DO: Use approved color variations
✗ DON'T: Change colors outside brand palette
✓ DO: Maintain proper proportions

Additional Don'ts

  • Don't recreate or redraw the logo
  • Don't rotate or skew the logo
  • Don't separate the path from the mountain
  • Don't add effects (shadows, gradients, outlines)
  • Don't change the typeface
  • Don't alter proportions or spacing
  • Don't use below minimum sizes
  • Don't animate without brand team approval

Brand Vocabulary

Use These Terms

Term Definition Usage Example
Basecamps Full-service hub locations with 6-8 DCFC, café, clean restrooms, indoor lounge "Visit our Baker Basecamp for charging and hospitality"
Waystations Satellite locations with 4 DCFC, indoor lounge, automated retail "Find our Waystation network along the scenic corridor"
Trailkeepers Our staff members at Basecamp locations "Our Trailkeepers are here to help with route planning"
Pathfinders Members of our Pathfinder Rewards loyalty program "Pathfinders enjoy flat rates with no time-of-use surprises"
Scenic corridors The highway routes we serve connecting National Parks and attractions "We're building America's charging network for scenic corridors"

Avoid These Terms

  • "Gas station" (we're not replicating that model)
  • "Charging station" alone (use "Basecamp" or "Waystation")
  • "Customers" (prefer "travelers," "members," or "Pathfinders")
  • "Urban network" (we focus on scenic corridors, not cities)
  • "Fast food" (we partner with quality regional providers)

Writing Style

✓ Good Example

"Charge where the journey matters. Our Baker Basecamp offers indoor comfort while you explore Death Valley, with Pathfinder flat rates that never surprise you."

✗ Poor Example

"Visit our charging station for fast DC charging. Customers can access amenities. Standard rates apply with time-of-use pricing."

File Format Notes

SVG (Scalable Vector Graphics) is the preferred format for all applications. SVG files scale infinitely without loss of quality and work across web, print, and digital applications.

If you need other formats (PNG, PDF, EPS), export from the SVG files at your required resolution. For print, always use vector formats when possible.
