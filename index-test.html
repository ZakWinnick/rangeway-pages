Common Questions

When are you opening?

Our first locations open in 2026 across California, Colorado, Nevada, Utah, and Hawaii. Sign up to get notified when we launch near you.

What EVs do you support?

Every Waystation has CCS and NACS connectors. We work with every EV on the road.

How fast is the charging?

We use ultra-fast DC charging. Most EVs can charge from 20% to 80% in 20-30 minutes—just enough time to grab a coffee and stretch your legs.

Is it free to use the lounge?

Yes. Every charging customer has full access to our climate-controlled lounges, WiFi, and restrooms at no extra charge.
