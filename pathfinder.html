Member Benefits

More Than Just Credits Back

Pathfinder Rewards is a true hospitality loyalty program - like Marriott Bonvoy or Delta SkyMiles. Earn status through your loyalty, unlock experiences that go beyond transactions.

⚡

Earned Status Recognition

Your status is displayed when you connect. Lounge displays welcome you by name. Staff knows your tier. You're recognized for your loyalty, not your payment.

💰

Credits Back on Every Charge

Voyager earns 5%, Pioneer earns 10%, Trailblazer earns 15% credits back. Plus annual milestone credits, birthday credits, and seasonal bonuses.

🎯

Exclusive Experiences

Pioneer member events, Trailblazer Summit, Basecamp grand openings - status unlocks experiences money can't buy.

⭐

Status That Lasts

Once earned, your status is valid through the end of the following calendar year. No monthly fees. No subscriptions. Just rewards for your loyalty.

🤝

Partner Perks Network

10-15% off at partner hotels, private tour access at attractions, pro-deals on outdoor gear and EV accessories. Your status unlocks curated partnerships.

👤

Concierge Relationship

Trailblazers get a dedicated relationship manager who remembers your preferences, plans your trips, and handles issues proactively.

Our Network

From Coast to Mountains

Experience the diversity of America's scenic highways. Our charging network spans desert corridors, mountain passes, and coastal routes.

Desert Corridors

From the Mojave to the Sonoran, our stations dot the vast desert landscapes where wide-open roads meet endless horizons. Experience the stark beauty of America's arid regions while staying charged and comfortable.

Mountain Passes

Navigate alpine highways with confidence. Our high-elevation stations provide reliable charging through the Cascades, Sierra Nevada, and beyond. Adventure awaits where the mountains meet the sky.

Coastal Highways

Cruise the legendary Pacific Coast Highway and beyond with ocean views and sea breezes. Our coastal stations offer the perfect stop to recharge while taking in dramatic cliffside vistas and endless blue horizons.
